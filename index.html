GALLERYMEMORIES

DUBAI

Dubai is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the capital of the Emirate of Dubai, the most populated of the country's seven.The emirate's capital, also named Dubai, is the largest city of the federation. The city is located on a small creek in the northeast part of the state.

LONDON

London is the capital city of the United Kingdom. It is the U.K.'s largest metropolis and its economic, transportation, and cultural centre. London is also among the oldest of the world's great cities, with its history spanning nearly two millennia.

PARIS

Paris has a long history of being associated with love and romance. The city has been the backdrop for countless romantic novels, films, and songs, solidifying its reputation as a destination for lovers. And let's not forget about the iconic Eiffel Tower, which has become a symbol of love and romance around the world.

SOUTHAFRICA

South Africa is the world's biggest producer of gold, platinum, chromium, vanadium, manganese and alumino-silicates. It also produces nearly 40% of the world's chrome and vermiculite. Durban is the largest port in Africa and the ninth largest in the world. South Africa generates two-thirds of Africa's electricity.

GERMANY

Germany, located in the heart of Europe with 16 states, is famous for being the largest economy in Europe as well as one of the largest in the world. Its captivating history is told through its old-fashioned architecture, castles, palaces, cathedrals, and natural landscapes.

INDIA

India is the world's seventh-largest country and most populated country. It's located on the Indian tectonic plate, which is part of the Indo-Australian Plate. India is a megadiverse country with a high level of biological diversity.
